Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


  • How do I schedule a consultation?
    You can book a consultation using the form above. We will contact you to confirm the time.

  • Do I need a prescription for a consultation?
    No, a prescription is not required for general consultations. However, for specific medication-related advice, we may ask for a prescription.

  • Can I consult online?
    Yes, we offer online consultations via video calls. You can choose this option when filling out the booking form.

  • What should I bring to my consultation?
    Please bring a list of your current medications (if any) and any relevant medical records or concerns you'd like to discuss.
